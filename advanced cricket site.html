Team Overview

The Indian national cricket team, also known as Team India and Men in Blue, is governed by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI). It is a full member of the International Cricket Council (ICC) with Test, One Day International (ODI), and Twenty20 International (T20I) status.
